    If you’re interested in using the tools and equipment in the creatorspace, you can get certified through the Slugworks Canvas Course available on our website. We recently reorganized modules and added sections for each part of the creatorspace. You’ll learn about each room in Slugworks, where different tools are kept, and safety procedures.

    The Slugworks Canvas course now supports badges! As you complete each module, you’ll receive a new badge that will let Slugworks staff know which rooms/tools you’re able to use.


    Accessing Slugworks BE 51 (Machine Shop):

    CMPM 115: Lead by Design:

    Hi everyone,

    Happy New Year and welcome to winter quarter! We’re excited to start the new year with you. Here is some new information you should know, as well as some exciting opportunities coming up:

    slugworks.engineering.ucsc.edu

    Do you have an idea or skill that you would like to develop and share with others? CMPM 115, Lead By Design, gives you the opportunity to do that! Through this course, you will be guided through the process of developing a 3 unit engineering project-based design lab. You will then be paid to teach the course to first year students in the following quarter. CMPM 115 requires an application and is tailored to upper division students. It will be offered in Spring 2024, and students are encouraged to apply in groups. If you have any questions, contact Tela Favaloro at tfalavor@ucsc.edu.
